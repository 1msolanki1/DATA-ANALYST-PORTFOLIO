<h2>Store Sales Dashboard</h2>
       <h3>Problem Statement:</h3>
The primary objective of this analysis was to equip the store with actionable insights derived from the sales data of 2022. By comprehensively understanding customer behavior and sales dynamics, the store aimed to pave the way for strategic decision-making and growth initiatives in 2023. Specifically, the store sought to address questions pertaining to sales trends, customer demographics, regional contributions, and channel performance. By addressing these queries, the store aimed to optimize its operations, enhance customer engagement, and maximize profitability in the coming year.



<img width="814" alt="Store Dashbaord" src="https://github.com/msolanki1/DATA-ANALYST-PORTFOLIO/assets/144268701/de8e1538-a285-4fb1-9ac0-157864bdf13b">


<h3>Data Structure:</h3>
        The dataset provided for analysis comprised a structured format encompassing various attributes pertinent to sales transactions. Each entry in the dataset contained key information such as Order ID, Customer ID, Gender, Age, Date, Status, Channel, SKU (Stock Keeping Unit), Category, Quantity, Currency, Amount, Shipping Details, and B2B (Business-to-Business). This structured schema facilitated systematic analysis and enabled the extraction of valuable insights regarding sales patterns, customer demographics, and operational efficiency.

<h3>Sample Questions:</h3>
        The analysis was guided by a set of sample questions designed to elucidate different facets of the store's sales performance and customer behavior. These questions included:
            <ul>
                <li>How do sales and orders correlate, as depicted in a single chart?</li>
                <li>Which month witnessed the highest sales volume and order frequency?</li>
                <li>What are the gender-based purchasing trends, and which gender tends to make more purchases?</li>
                <li>How do order statuses vary across different years, and what implications does this have on operational efficiency?</li>
                <li>Can we identify the top 10 states contributing to overall sales, and what factors might influence their performance?</li>
                <li>Is there a discernible relationship between age groups and gender concerning sales volume?</li>
                <li>Which sales channel contributes the most to overall sales, and how does its performance compare to others?</li>
                <li>What product category emerges as the highest-selling, and what strategies can be devised to capitalize on its popularity?</li>
            </ul>
        

 <h3>Data Cleaning:</h3>
        The initial step involved rigorous scrutiny of the dataset to identify and rectify any anomalies. This included checking for null values and eliminating duplicate entries. Additionally, standardization efforts were implemented to ensure consistency across data points. For instance, variations in gender categorizations such as 'm' and 'men' were unified into a single, standardized format ('Men' or 'Women').

 <h3>Data Processing:</h3>
        Following data cleaning, further processing was conducted to enhance the dataset's usability and relevance. Notable transformations included the creation of an 'Age Group' column, which categorized customers into distinct age brackets based on their age. Furthermore, the extraction of month information from the 'Date' column facilitated temporal analysis and seasonality insights.

 <h3>Data Analysis:</h3>
        Leveraging the refined dataset, a comprehensive analysis was performed to unveil patterns, trends, and correlations. Various analytical approaches were employed, ranging from simple descriptive statistics to more advanced predictive modeling techniques. Key areas of analysis included sales trends by order ID, gender-based purchasing behavior, age-related sales patterns, top contributing states, order status dynamics over time, and the impact of different sales channels.</p>

  <h3>Data Visualization:</h3>
        To facilitate better comprehension and decision-making, the insights derived from the analysis phase were translated into visually appealing and interactive representations. Graphical representations, such as bar charts, line graphs, pie charts, and heat maps, were utilized to encapsulate complex data relationships concisely. These visualizations were then integrated into a unified dashboard, providing stakeholders with a holistic view of the store's sales performance.
